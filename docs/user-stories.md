# ComplyAI — User Stories
## Domain: Hiring AI — Resume Screening
## Framework: EU AI Act

---

## Epic 1 — Risk Classification

**US-01**
As a product founder, I want to know immediately whether my resume screening AI is classified as High Risk under the EU AI Act, so that I understand whether full compliance obligations apply to me.

**US-02**
As a product founder, I want to see the exact legal basis for my risk classification (article, annex, section), so that I can verify it and share it with my legal counsel without ambiguity.

**US-03**
As a product founder, I want to know my compliance deadline by jurisdiction, so that I understand when I need to be fully compliant and can plan accordingly.

**US-04**
As a non-lawyer, I want the risk classification explained in plain English, so that I can understand what it means for my business without needing to read the full regulation.

---

## Epic 2 — Intake Form

**US-05**
As a user, I want to complete the compliance intake in under 5 minutes, so that I get value quickly without a lengthy onboarding process.

**US-06**
As a user, I want the intake questions to use plain language rather than legal terminology, so that I can answer accurately without a legal background.

**US-07**
As a user, I want to be able to say "not sure" to a question, so that uncertainty is captured honestly rather than forcing me to guess.

**US-08**
As a returning user, I want to update individual intake answers without restarting the whole form, so that I can reflect fixes my team has made without losing previous answers.

**US-09**
As a user, I want to see a progress indicator during the intake, so that I know how many questions remain and do not abandon the flow.

---

## Epic 3 — Gap Analysis

**US-10**
As a product founder, I want to see all my compliance gaps in one dashboard ranked by severity, so that I know what to fix first without having to read through a legal document.

**US-11**
As a product founder, I want each gap to cite the exact EU AI Act article and sub-clause it relates to, so that I can verify the requirement and share it with confidence.

**US-12**
As a product founder, I want each gap to include a plain-English explanation of what is wrong, so that I understand the issue without legal interpretation.

**US-13**
As a product founder, I want each gap to include a concrete fix recommendation, so that I can hand it directly to my CTO or engineering team as an actionable task.

**US-14**
As a CTO receiving a shared report, I want to understand what needs to be built or changed technically, so that I can estimate effort and prioritise the work.

**US-15**
As a user, I want to see gaps grouped by severity (Critical, High, Medium), so that I can focus limited resources on the most legally urgent issues first.

**US-16**
As a user, I want to expand and collapse individual gap cards, so that I can focus on one gap at a time without being overwhelmed by the full list.

**US-17**
As a user, I want to see which gaps I have already resolved update in real time when I update my intake answers, so that I can track progress clearly.

---

## Epic 4 — Audit Report PDF

**US-18**
As a product founder, I want to download a professionally formatted PDF audit report, so that I can share it with customers, investors, or legal counsel as a credible compliance artifact.

**US-19**
As a product founder, I want the PDF to include my company name and system description, so that it is clearly attributed to my product and not a generic template.

**US-20**
As a product founder, I want the PDF to include a prioritised fix list, so that whoever receives it can see not just what is wrong but what to do about it.

**US-21**
As a product founder, I want the PDF to include compliance deadlines per jurisdiction, so that the recipient understands the urgency without needing to look up dates separately.

**US-22**
As a product founder, I want to regenerate the PDF after updating my intake answers, so that I always have a report that reflects my current compliance status.

---

## Epic 5 — Account and Progress Tracking

**US-23**
As a returning user, I want to log back into my account and see my previous intake and gap analysis, so that I do not have to start from scratch each visit.

**US-24**
As a user, I want to see a compliance score or progress indicator that improves as I resolve gaps, so that I feel a sense of progress and have a metric to report internally.

**US-25**
As a user, I want to see a history of my previous reports with dates, so that I can show an auditor or customer that I have been actively improving compliance over time.

**US-26**
As a product founder, I want to sign up with my work email and no credit card, so that I can evaluate the product without a financial commitment.

---

## Epic 6 — Sharing and Collaboration

**US-27**
As a product founder, I want to share a link to my gap analysis dashboard with my Head of Legal or CTO, so that they can review it without me having to export and email a document.

**US-28**
As a Head of Legal reviewing a shared report, I want to see the full citation for every gap, so that I can independently verify each requirement against the source regulation.

---

## Compliance Rules Coverage
All user stories above map to the 88 EU AI Act rules applicable to:
- High Risk AI systems under Annex III, Section 4(a)
- Resume screening and candidate ranking tools
- Obligations on **providers** (companies that build the AI)
- Obligations on **deployers** (companies that use the AI in hiring)

Key articles covered: 4, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 26, 27, 43, 48
